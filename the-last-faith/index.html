Mythringal rises

Discover the secrets of a decadent world where an ancient religion is the law. Face horrific beasts to subvert an hidden prophecy.

The game

The Last Faith is an exploration-based Metroidvania, action side-scrolling platformer with dark and gothic themes at its core.

The features

The main features of the game are, satisfying reactive action combat system, a high number of different weapons, use of the special buffs to power up you weapons, a variety of magic spells to support your combats, a giant world with terrific environments, and a highly detailed pixel art style.
